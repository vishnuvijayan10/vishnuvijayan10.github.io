About us

Design. Build. Inspire.

Established with the vision of providing sustainable and qualified professional expertise in the EPC industry, Interia is one of the thriving interior fit-out and contract furnishing companies in the UAE. We deliver projects across a multitude of sectors, including hospitality, residential, and commercial, for some of the world's most recognizable brands, companies, and operators.

The company demonstrates a strong commitment to quality, reflected in its professional management team, state-of-the-art joinery facility, and highly skilled craftsmen. Interia has undertaken numerous prestigious projects in the UAE, ranging from luxury five-star hotel fit-outs to commercial offices and luxurious residential developments.
